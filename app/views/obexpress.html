We need evidence to support your assessment

To complete your assessment application we need to know how much you earn, how much you spend, and whether you’re getting any benefits.

To collect some of the evidence we need to connect to your bank and other government departments.

Evidence we can collect for means assessment:

  • How much you earn (income)
  • Any Childcare payments
  • Private Pension
  • Employer pension
  • Board or rent payments
  • Maintainance payments
  • Housing costs

Evidence we can collect for capital assessment:

  • Bank savings
  • Bank or building society account balance
  • ISA held at your bank
