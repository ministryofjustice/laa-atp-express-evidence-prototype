Assessing your application

To assess your application for legal aid we need to know:

  • how much you earn
  • how much you spend
  • if you are getting any benefits - like housing benefit or universal credit

We will use any evidence you provide to support your application for legal aid

See more about the information we collect

To help us work out how much you can afford, we'll collect information about:

  • How much you earn (income)
  • Any Childcare payments
  • Private Pension
  • Employer pension
  • Board or rent payments
  • Maintainance payments
  • Housing costs

To help us work out how much you have saved, we'll collect information about:

  • Bank savings
  • Bank or building society account balance
  • ISA held at your bank
