Select transactions on your bank statement

We need to know about regular incoming payments (such as wages, benefits or pension) and major outgoings (such as childcare, mortgage, rent or housing costs).

We do not need to know about smaller, everday purchases.

How to choose your transactions

  1. Check the box next to the item on your bank statement
  2. Select the type of transaction (e.g. salary or childcare)
  3. Select how often you receive or pay out money (e.g. weekly or monthly)
  4. Choose all relevant items and click 'Continue'
